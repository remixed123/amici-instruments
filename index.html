Manifesto

Workflow is the instrument.

Meet Marco — 4-Oscillator Synth with Independent Shaping and Dual Hard Sync → Meet Bruno — Powerful Layered Drum and Pluck Synthesizer →

Most software synthesizers honour hardware — the knobs, panels and layouts inherited from the machines musicians grew up with. There's wonderful work being done there, and we love it. We just wanted to explore what becomes possible when digital lets go of those physical constraints.

So we decided to start somewhere else.

Vintage sounds are wonderful — Marco and Bruno make them happily too. But for us, sound isn't the frontier. Workflow is.

When nothing has to fit on a physical panel, the question changes:

What if workflow was the design — and the sound followed?

That changes everything.

It leads to instruments that use space the way only digital can. A random generator that produces musical sounds, not chaos. A tweak system that doesn't just kick off your sound design — it becomes part of the writing itself.

Discovery as a workflow.
Tweaking as a writing tool.
New ideas — like Dual Hard Sync — emerging because the way music gets made is finally free to invent them.

Amici means friends in Italian — and that's what our apps are meant to be. They have first names too: Marco. Bruno. Colourful, a little bit toy-like, eager to play. Pick one up and it feels inviting from the very first touch.

Every Amici instrument is deep enough for an experienced sound designer, immediate enough for someone touching synthesis for the first time.

Not simplified. Not watered down. Designed to feel alive the moment you touch it.

iOS music has its own future to write.

It should be its own creative world.

MARCO

4-Oscillator Synth with Independent Shaping and Dual Hard Sync

Four fully independent oscillators — each with its own waveform, filter, envelopes and modulation — driving two hard-synced voices. A musical random generator and a Tweak system that's part of the writing — sounds nobody else can make, from a synth anyone can play. Available as a standalone app and an AUv3 plugin.

Drum & Pluck Synthesizer · iPhone, iPad & Mac

BRUNO

A drum machine that thinks like a synth.

Six voices, four synthesis engines per drum. Stack Analog, FM, Fold and Resonant the way the pros build big drums — for a punchy, mix-ready sound. Roll the dice on any engine, then tweak: a fast, musical workflow that keeps fresh ideas coming. Available as a standalone app and an AUv3 plugin.
